Which two websites are you most inspired by?

The two sites, I was most inspired by were:

  • Always Creative Design Studio
  • This is Now Exhibition

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size, or balance?

Always Creative Design Studio

The Always Creative Design Studio page renders with a clear focal point in the center of the page, and a simple background. By centering their brand and location, the studio provides a clear message at initial view of the website. Scrolling through the rest of the site, Creative Design utilizes clear lines and a combination of square/rectangle focal points.

This is Now

This Is Now Exhibition opens with a full page layout. Each topic/theme takes up an entire page, and as the user scrolls, their flow stays the same. Due to a variety of fonts and focal points, the design suggests a lacks coherence or organization, and instead a free flow of ideas.

What makes them similar and different?

Both designs represent a modular design with geometric focal points throughout the page. Although the Now Exhibition is a full page frame, the Always Creative Design Studio focuses on smaller frames. In my opinion, I believe the Creative Design Studio does a better job in curating both its content and its user.
