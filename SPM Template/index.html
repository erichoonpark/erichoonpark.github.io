SPM Overview
We’ve designed an entire Sales Performance Management software suite that is centered on improving the sales people themselves, with the intent of creating a more performant sales organization. We accomplish this by providing sales management a single solution, available onsite or in the cloud, to better manage, better motivate, and better mentor their sales people so that they can ultimately blow out their numbers.

Suite of Applications
Composed of eight seperate modules, SPM is a suite of applications that has been designed to create a more performant sales organization. By layering technologies on top of an organization's best practices, sales managers can better manage, motivate, and mentor their sales personnel. Oracle's offering provides end-to-end capabilities, including territory planning, quota modeling, incentives and games, sales coaching, and more. Each module of SPM can be taken and customized seperately or together to best fit your needs.
